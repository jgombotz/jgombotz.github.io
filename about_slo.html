San Luis Obispo, CA
is a tourist destination located on the Central Coast, halfway between LA and San Francisco. It has a small airport that services direct flights to LA, San Francisco, Seattle and Phoenix with hundreds of connecting flights. There is also train service starting from San Diego, CA and north to Bend, Oregon. The area boasts year-round temperatures in the 60’s-70’s, the ocean, mountains and over 300 wineries.

Hotel Recommendations
There are two, large hotels currently under construction slated for completion in April 2018, and several other boutique hotels like the Granada Inn, Apple Farm Inn, and the famous Madonna Inn to house guests. SLO also has main-stay accommodations such as Embassy Suites.
